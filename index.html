How to Edit Your Student Essay (Free Guide)

Editing is the secret weapon of top students. Here’s how to do it right:

1. Read It Aloud

Your ear catches mistakes your eyes miss. Awkward phrasing? Fix it.

2. Kill Filler Words

Remove: “very,” “really,” “in order to,” “due to the fact that.” Be direct.

3. Check Your Thesis

Is your main argument clear in the first paragraph? If not, rewrite it.
